Dina Lunken DDS Privacy Policy
Effective Date: Effective Date:01/14/2026
Dina Lunken DDS ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our SMS notification service and other related patient communications.
1. Information We Collect
We may collect and maintain the following types of information:
•	Contact information such as your name, mobile phone number, and email address
•	Appointment and scheduling information
•	Account and billing details necessary for patient communications
•	Any other information you submit to us via text, online forms, or in-person
2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information strictly for the following purposes:
•	To send appointment reminders and notifications
•	To inform you of balances due on your account
•	To remind you to schedule appointments
•	To deliver required information about your dental care (such as forms)
•	To respond to your inquiries or requests
Your information will not be used for promotional or marketing purposes unless you provide explicit consent.
3. Sharing of Information
We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only with trusted service providers who support our SMS and communication services, and only as necessary for those services. We may also disclose information if required by law.
4. Protection of Information
We implement reasonable security practices to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
5. Your Choices
You may opt out of our SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. If you wish to update or delete your personal information from our systems, please contact our office at (815) 223-6622.
